## Cropping Notes

- Use the sapphire hero with the gemstone pushed right or lower-right, leaving dark negative space for centered text.
- Use gemstone cutout PNGs inside dark card backgrounds; this keeps them aligned with the black velvet luxury direction even when the source has transparency.
- Avoid using the bright panoramic gemstone images without a dark overlay.
- Keep service image cards at 4:5 ratio for a luxury editorial grid.
- On mobile, crop service cards center-center and keep text overlays at the bottom.

## Performance Notes

- Oversized PNGs were copied into `assets-for-upload` and reduced to a maximum dimension of 2400px where needed.
- Smaller source files were not upscaled.
- Squarespace will generate responsive derivatives after upload.
- Use JPG/WebP exports later if you need smaller file sizes, but keep transparent PNGs for gemstone cutouts unless replacing backgrounds.
