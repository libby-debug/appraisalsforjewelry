## Template Recommendation

Best starting point: Squarespace 7.1 using a Professional Services > Consultants template, then rebuild the homepage with Fluid Engine sections.

Recommended template direction: Clove, or the closest current Professional Services/Consultants template with a full-width hero image, strong white space, editorial typography, and simple service-page navigation. Clove is a strong fit because it starts from a polished, white-space-forward professional layout; in Squarespace 7.1, the exact starter template matters less than choosing a calm template and rebuilding sections with Fluid Engine.

Why this works:
- The business is a luxury advisory service, not a retail jewelry store.
- Professional Services/Consultants keeps the structure trustworthy and appointment-led.
- Fluid Engine makes each section easy to edit visually later.
- Native Form, Gallery, Summary, Accordion, Map, and Scheduling blocks keep maintenance simple.

- Avoid bright white product cutouts, casual lifestyle photos, busy retail layouts, and ecommerce-style product grids.
- Keep image crops cinematic, close, dark, and textural.

Mobile typography targets:
- Hero H1: 42-54px mobile, 86-112px desktop
- Section H2: 34-44px mobile, 60-78px desktop
- Body: 16-18px
- Button text: 12-13px uppercase with generous letter spacing

### 7. FAQ Section

Squarespace section type: Accordion block.

Background: #F8F6F2.

H2:
Appraisal FAQ

Accordion items:

How long does an appraisal take?
Most single-item appointments take 30 to 60 minutes. Larger collections may require additional review time and a scheduled delivery date for completed documentation.

Do I need an appointment?
Yes. Appointments allow each appraisal to be handled privately, with enough time reserved for examination, photographs, measurements, and questions.

What should I bring?
Bring the jewelry or watch, previous appraisals, grading reports, receipts, insurance records, provenance notes, and any supporting documentation you may have.

How often should jewelry be appraised?
For insurance purposes, many clients update appraisals every three to five years, or sooner after major market changes, restoration, inheritance, or acquisition.

Are appraisals confidential?
Yes. Client details, collection information, values, and supporting documents are handled with discretion and professional confidentiality.

## Image Plan

Overall image direction:
- Use exclusively high-end luxury imagery featuring diamonds, loose gemstones, antique jewelry, fine rings, elegant necklaces, luxury watches, macro gemstone photography, and sparkling diamond closeups.
- Imagery should be predominantly black or very dark, with dramatic luxury lighting and high contrast.
- Look for black velvet surfaces, cinematic shadows, refined reflections, tight macro detail, and gemstone sparkle.
- The visual world should reference Sotheby's-style jewelry auctions, black velvet jewelry campaigns, high-end diamond editorials, fine gemstone macro photography, and luxury watch advertising.
- Avoid bright white stock backgrounds, casual lifestyle photography, colorful boutique imagery, generic retail jewelry-store photos, and cheap ecommerce cutout layouts.
